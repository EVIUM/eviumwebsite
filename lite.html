Legend

1 million years after the big bang, a large celestial body known as EVIUM, originating from deep interstellar space, was pulled into orbit by a large gas giant planet. The powerful gas giant was orbiting a binary star system in one of the first galaxies to form. EVIUM, rich in minerals, formed an atmosphere and the EVIUM God flower sprouted from the soil. The flower, shaped like a sunflower gazed at its newly surrounding celestial bodies, and wondered at the constellations in the night sky. Over billions of years, the flower absorbed all of the solar energy that it could from the star system, and mystical, dark energies from the gas giant and surrounding universe. The flower developed sentience, and started manifesting EVIUM creatures that will roam EVIUM as hunter gatherers. The god flower gave rise to 7 Energy types which are: Alpha, Beta, Gamma, Delta, Phi, Psi, & Omega, and the flower adopted Delta as its own energy. 154 seeds began to grow from the flower, from which 7 energy eggs and 147 EVIUM lifeforms were conceived.
